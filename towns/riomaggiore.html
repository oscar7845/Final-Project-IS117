Riomaggiore

Riomaggiore is the most southern village of the Cinque Terre, situated just a few minutes by train from La Spezia. The town climbs up along the ridges overlooking the sea and it is characterized from the typical stone houses with coloured façades and slate-roofs.

In the small picturesque village of Riomaggiore you can find some romantic guesthouses with fantastic sea views. There are also some charming hotels with great location in the centre of the small town.

In Riomaggiore starts the Via dell’Amore, Lover’s Lane, a wonderful path known for the romantic atmosphere and amazing landscapes. It leads to Manarola and it takes twenty minutes walking. Unfortunately the path is closed following a landslide. It is now being repaired and will be reopened in 2024.

An alternative path from Riomaggiore to Manarola is Via Beccara (1h) which is a vigorous hike, and therefore not for the faint of heart. The Beccara trail goes straight up over the top of the mountain and down the other side. This trail is free.

You can also walk the trail from Riomaggiore to Portovenere (5 hours). During this excursion you will see breathtaking panoramic sea views right next to the cliffs. You do not need a trekking card for this hike.
